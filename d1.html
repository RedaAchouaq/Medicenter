Welcome In Your Clinic

Visit my clinic every 6 months for a professional cleaning and check-up.
Your oral health is an important part of your overall health, and regular visits allow me to detect and treat issues early.

Dear patients,
To keep your smile healthy and beautiful, I encourage you to:
Brush your teeth twice a day with fluoride toothpaste.
Floss daily to clean between your teeth.
Limit sugar and acidic drinks, and drink plenty of water.
Don’t ignore sensitivity or pain—small problems can become serious if untreated.
